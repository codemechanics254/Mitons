What we do

innovative food safety and quality service provider to the food,
hospitality and horticultural industry

We provide high quality and cost effective service to allow food businesses to exceed their customer’s expectations through innovative solutions to food safety
Our goal is to help our clients to manage food safety, quality and legality within their business, whilst adding value to the operation and allowing our clients to realize their goals.
Our client base employs MITONS LTD to oversee a range of technical functions from compliance audits, specification writing, and label checking to full management of their quality management systems.
We have highly qualified and experienced consultants on hand to advise your business on all issues relating to food safety, quality and legality.

Vision

To be the food safety systems consulting company of choice locally, regionally and internationally.

Standards

We help you select the best GFSI food safety standard for your business, the one that will enhance your marketability and grow bottom line profits while mitigating your risk.

Mission

To have an outstanding reputation for excellence in food safety and quality issues and to provide the best food science professionals in the industry.

We offer training, documentation and Implementation of food safety and quality management systems including:

  • Food Safety System – FSSC 22000
  • Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point - (HACCP)
  • Food Safety Management System - ISO 22000
  • Good manufacturing practices
  • Prerequisite programmes – ISO/TS 22002
  • Food and drink defense – PAS 96:2014
  • BRC Global Standard for Food Safety
  • Food products research and development
  • Nutrition for health improvement
  • Quality ManagementSystems (QMS)- ISO 9000
  • Crop Protection
  • Global Gap, Kenya GAP
  • Ethical production – Fair Trade
  • Organicstandards in production and processing
  • Customers specific standards
  • Food safety and quality systems audits
  • Food Fortification
  • Specific technologies of food
  • Quality assurance: Laboratory management, quality analysis and recording, sensory evaluation & statistical quality control
